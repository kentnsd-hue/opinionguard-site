A/B Testing Playbook

  • Start with Ads 1, 3, and 5 — they cover guarantee, social proof, and direct approaches. Different enough to identify what resonates.
  • Budget: $20-30/day split across 3 ads. Let them run 3-5 days before making decisions.
  • Kill metric: If CTR is below 1% after 1,000 impressions, kill it. If cost-per-click is above $5, kill it.
  • Scale metric: If cost-per-lead is under $50, increase budget. Under $30 = scale aggressively.
  • Audience: Target HVAC business owners, ages 28-65, US only. Interests: HVAC, heating and cooling, ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro, home services business owner.
  • Creative pairing: Ad 1 + ad_1.png (guarantee creative). Ad 3 + ad_2.png (before/after creative). Ad 5 + either.
  • Retargeting: After 7 days, create a retargeting audience of people who clicked but didn't sign up. Run Ads 2 and 4 to this warm audience — the emotional and reactivation angles work better on people who already know what you do.
  • Rotate creative every 2 weeks to avoid ad fatigue. Keep winning copy, change the image/video.
