Questions? Answers.

How does the 20-review guarantee work?

Upload at least 200 past customer contacts when you set up. If you don't have 20 new Google reviews within 30 days, your entire first month is free. No fine print, no forms to fill out — we just don't charge you.

How long does setup actually take?

About 10 minutes. Connect your Google Business Profile, upload your customer list (we have export guides for QuickBooks, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, and Jobber), and customize your message. No meetings required. If you'd prefer someone to walk you through it, our Pro and Premium plans include setup help via text/email.

What if a customer is unhappy?

That's exactly what OpinionGuard is designed for. Customers who rate you 1-7 get routed to a private feedback form — not Google. You get an instant text alert so you can reach out and fix the issue before it becomes a public review. It's better customer service AND better reputation management.

Does this work with my CRM or invoicing software?

Yes. We integrate directly with QuickBooks, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, Workiz, Square, and thousands more via Zapier. If your software isn't listed, you can simply upload a CSV — we provide a simple template with just 3 columns (Name, Phone, Email).

Is there a contract?

No. Month-to-month, cancel anytime with a click in your dashboard. We're easy to work with and easy to get rid of — but our customers stick around because they see results every month.

Won't customers get annoyed by the texts?

Our messages are sent once after service — when satisfaction is highest. They're friendly and personal, with your company name. Follow-ups are gentle and spaced out. We've optimized our templates based on what actually gets reviews without annoying people.

What about the $5/month SMS cost mentioned in pricing?

Text messages and emails have a small carrier cost. Rather than bake it into a higher price, we pass through the actual cost — typically under $5/month depending on your customer volume. We don't mark it up or make money on it. Most customers don't even notice it.

Is review gating against Google's policy?

We don't gate reviews — every customer gets asked. The rating step helps you identify unhappy customers so you can address their concerns personally, which is better customer service. Happy customers get directed to Google. Google encourages businesses to ask all customers for reviews, and that's what we do.

How many reviews should I expect?

During the initial reactivation, expect 10-15% of past customers to leave a review. For new customers post-service, we typically see 20-30% review rates, with many HVAC companies seeing over 50%. Your results depend on your customer volume and the quality of your service — but if you're doing good work, the reviews will show it.
